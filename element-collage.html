Aspinwall attorney leads 'Spin Your Wheels' fundraiser at fitness center

We organized this fundraiser because disabilities affect all of us. Jennifer’s 15 year old son (Katherine’s godson), Luke has autism as well as cerebral palsy. Katherine is an elementary school special education teacher and works with children with various intellectual and physical disabilities every day. Andrea has close friends who have children with disabilities and has been an integral part of the behind the scenes organization of the first three Spin Your Wheels fundraisers. Rory owns The Sweat Shoppe and is graciously providing the necessary equipment for the event. She has a long history of volunteer work and encourages her two daughters to actively volunteer within the special needs community as well.
